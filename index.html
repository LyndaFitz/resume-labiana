Name: Alicia Silverstone

Date Of Birth: October 4, 1976

Profession: Actress

Skills: Improvisation, Voice Control, Screen Presence, Emotional Range, Character Development

Professional Summary:

Acclaimed American actress and environmental activist with over two decades of experience in film and television. Best known for her iconic role as Cher Horowitz in "Clueless." Proven advocate for environmental and animal rights, including authoring works on plant-based living. Demonstrates strong commitment to sustainability and social impact through both professional and personal endeavors.

Employment:

"Clueless" (1995) – As Cher Horowitz, the role that made her a teen icon. "The Crush" (1993) – Her breakout film as a teenager obsessed with an older man. "Batman & Robin" (1997) – Played Batgirl/Barbara Wilson. "Blast from the Past" (1999) – Co-starred as Eve Rustikoff in this romantic comedy. "Excess Baggage" (1997) – Starred as a spoiled rich girl who fakes her own kidnapping. "The Babysitter's Club" (1995) – Appeared as a guest in this film based on the popular book series. "Butter" (2011) – Played a former beauty queen in this satirical comedy.
